# QL_W

## PI: Qing Wang, Dawei Wang and Chengyuan Song

* The dataset are following the protocal of PL_G and PL_S. This study was approved by the Medical Ethics Committee of Qilu Hospital of Shandong University. The patients were recruited from the memory clinic of the neurology department of Department of Neurology and Radiology, Qilu Hospital of Shandong University, Jiâ€™nan, China. The control subjects were recruited from the local community using advertisements. Written informed consent was obtained from each enrolled subject or his/her authorized guardian. The participants underwent general physical, psychological and laboratory examinations prior to enrollment in the formal study. The participants did not take medications that might have influenced cognition during the scans, and all patients received professional suggestions for further treatment.
